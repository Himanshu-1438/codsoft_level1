Neeraj Chopra

India's Golden Boy of Athletics

Biography

Neeraj Chopra, born on December 24, 1997, in Khandra, Haryana, is an Indian track and field athlete and Olympic gold medalist in javelin throw. He made history by becoming the first Indian to win a gold medal in athletics at the Tokyo 2020 Olympics with a throw of 87.58 meters. Raised in a farming family, Neeraj's journey from a small village to global stardom is an inspiration to millions.
